# Product

## Register

product

## Users

LocalAI serves developers, infrastructure operators, and administrators who run
and manage self-hosted AI systems. They install and configure models and
backends, observe active work, troubleshoot deployments, and operate LocalAI
across personal hardware, servers, and distributed clusters.

## Product Purpose

LocalAI is an open-source AI engine for running models across modalities and
hardware behind familiar APIs while keeping data and operational control in the
user's infrastructure. The product succeeds when operators can understand the
system's state, complete management workflows confidently, and run only the
components they need without hidden external dependencies.

## Brand Personality

Technical, direct, trustworthy. The voice is concise and specific, gives users
the operational detail needed to act, and communicates system behavior without
marketing gloss or unnecessary ceremony.

## Anti-references

Avoid glossy generic SaaS styling, decorative motion, and presentation that
obscures or oversimplifies system behavior. Avoid ambiguous progress, status
that relies on color alone, and interfaces that conceal consequential technical
details from operators.

## Design Principles

- Make system state legible and actionable.
- Preserve operator control and explain consequential behavior.
- Use familiar, consistent controls that disappear into the task.
- Surface technical detail progressively without sacrificing clarity.
- Respect the existing LocalAI visual language across every workflow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.1 AA. Support keyboard and screen-reader use, honor reduced-motion
preferences, and pair color with text or iconography for every meaningful
status. Keep interactive targets and focus states perceivable in both light and
dark themes.
